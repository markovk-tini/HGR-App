System requirements

  • Operating system: Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit).
  • Webcam: any USB or built-in camera at 720p/30fps or higher. Faster cameras reduce motion blur on gestures.
  • Microphone: required for voice commands and dictation. A headset or external mic works best; laptop/webcam mics are fine.
  • GPU (recommended): NVIDIA (CUDA 12.x) or any Vulkan-capable AMD/Intel GPU for fast voice transcription and grammar correction. CPU-only mode works but is slower.
  • RAM: 8 GB minimum, 16 GB recommended (voice and grammar models load together).
  • Disk space: about 6 GB free after install (includes whisper models and optional Qwen 3B grammar model).
  • First run: Windows may prompt to allow camera and microphone access — accept both.

How to install

  • Click Download for Windows to download the installer.
  • Open Touchless_Installer.exe after the download finishes.
  • Follow the setup prompts to install Touchless.
  • Launch Touchless from the desktop shortcut or Start menu after installation.
